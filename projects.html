Quick Exit

Quick Exit is an iPhone and Android application designed to save you time when riding Hong Kongs MTR.

Over 4 million people ride the MTR everyday and the biggest frustration when riding the MTR is when you arrive at your destination, but realize your exit is on the other side of the station. Quick Exit solves this by telling you which train and door to get on so when you arrive at your destination you're right in front of the escalator nearest to your exit.

On March 8th, Quick Exit was featured in Ezone (Hong Kong's leading tech magazine)
